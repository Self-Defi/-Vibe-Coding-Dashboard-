1. Purpose and Scope

This Commercial Use Exception Addendum (“Addendum”) defines the exclusive and limited circumstances under which commercial use of the Vibe Coded software, methodology, documentation, templates, or generated materials (collectively, the “Software”) is permitted.

Absent an approved exception under this Addendum, all commercial use is expressly prohibited.

This Addendum forms part of and is governed by the Vibe Coded License v0.9-proof-stable (“Base License”). In the event of conflict, this Addendum controls commercial usage.

⸻

2. Definition of Commercial Use

For purposes of this Addendum, Commercial Use includes, but is not limited to, any use of the Software or its outputs that directly or indirectly generates revenue, consideration, or commercial advantage.

Commercial Use includes, without limitation:
	•	Selling or licensing access to the Software or derivative works
	•	Offering paid consulting, coaching, advisory, or professional services primarily built on or dependent upon the Software
	•	Embedding the Software or generated outputs into a paid product, service, or SaaS offering
	•	White-labeling, rebranding, or redistributing the Software or its outputs
	•	Monetizing workflows, templates, artifacts, or documentation generated directly from the Software
	•	Using the Software as a customer-facing or operational core system in a for-profit business

⸻

3. Permitted Commercial Exceptions (Approval Required)

Commercial use may be permitted only with prior written approval from the copyright holder and only under one of the following exception categories.

Approval is discretionary and may include additional written terms.

⸻

3.1 Client-Specific Internal Deployments

Commercial use may be approved for internal use by a single organization or client, provided that:
	•	The Software is used solely for internal operations or prototyping
	•	The Software and outputs are not resold, sublicensed, or redistributed
	•	All Vibe Coded branding, logos, and product references are removed or replaced
	•	The client is informed that the Software is not an official Vibe Coded product

Typical permitted use cases include:
	•	Internal dashboards
	•	One-off operational proofs
	•	Process exploration or validation prototypes

⸻

3.2 Educational and Training Programs

Commercial use may be approved for educational or training contexts, provided that:
	•	The Software is used as part of a curriculum, workshop, or instructional program
	•	Participants receive no redistribution or sublicensing rights
	•	Branding is preserved or modified only as explicitly approved in writing
	•	The Software is not represented as the instructor’s proprietary system

⸻

3.3 Revenue-Share or Partnership Agreements

Commercial use may be approved under a formal partnership or licensing agreement, provided that:
	•	A written agreement defining scope, duration, and compensation exists
	•	Revenue sharing, licensing fees, or other consideration is explicitly documented
	•	Branding usage is expressly approved
	•	Intellectual property ownership and boundaries are clearly defined

⸻

4. Non-Transferability and Limitations

All commercial exceptions granted under this Addendum:
	•	Are non-transferable
	•	Are limited to the specific scope, purpose, and duration approved
	•	Do not establish precedent or implied permission for future use
	•	May be revoked at any time if terms are violated

⸻

5. Attribution Requirements (When Approved)

When a commercial exception is granted, attribution must follow one of the following formats, as specified in writing:
	•	“Built using Vibe Coded methodology (licensed use)”
	•	“Inspired by Vibe Coded (with permission)”

No logos, trademarks, or branding may be used unless explicitly authorized.

⸻

6. Prohibited Representations

Even when a commercial exception is granted, the following are strictly prohibited:
	•	Claiming ownership of the Vibe Coded methodology
	•	Representing the Software or outputs as an official Vibe Coded product
	•	Implying endorsement, partnership, or certification without written approval
	•	Using Vibe Coded branding outside the approved scope

⸻

7. Termination

Any violation of this Addendum immediately terminates the granted commercial exception and reverts usage to the Base License restrictions.

Termination does not limit any other remedies available under law or contract.

⸻

8. Requests for Commercial Exceptions

All requests for commercial exceptions must be submitted in writing to the copyright holder.

Approval is discretionary and may be denied without explanation.
